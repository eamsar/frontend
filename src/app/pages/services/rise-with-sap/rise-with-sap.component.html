Rise With SAP

RISE with SAP est une solution tout-en-un proposée par SAP pour accompagner les entreprises dans leur transformation digitale. Elle inclut un ensemble de services et d’outils pour migrer vers le cloud, optimiser les processus métiers, et adopter les innovations technologiques plus rapidement.

  • Migration vers SAP S/4HANA Cloud
  • Accès à Business Technology Platform (BTP)
  • Services de support et maintenance intégrés

SAP RISE can level-up how your organization does business. But with this massive undertaking across many areas of your business, there can be roadblocks. As CIOs and IT leaders evaluate whether digital transformation projects make sense for their organization, SAP security can often be viewed as a roadblock.
